About Us

Elizabeth Sharpe Potions was founded in the year 1398 of the Third Age. As any history buff would remind you, this was during the time of the Pan-Rhysian Witch Trials, one of the most violent and widespread incidents of witch burning. Our potions helped keep our society's witches safe from would-be witch burners. Without us, witches could very well have gone extinct. As such, we have a long and proud history of helping those at odds with the kingdom. As we move into a world where magic and technology are beginning to mix, we seek to preserve our time-honored traditions of potion making, though we do indulge in throwing a Molotov potion from time to time! If you have any interest in joining our staff, feel free to contact us! We are always looking for talented folks with a passion for magic!
